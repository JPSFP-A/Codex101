SECTION 3 · APPS & PLUGINS

What Are Plugins & How Do They Work?

Giving ChatGPT hands — the ability to reach into your real systems

🧠

Without a Plugin

ChatGPT in isolation

ChatGPT knows a great deal from its training — but it cannot see your live data. It can't check today's AR balance, send an email, or look up Invoice #1842. Brilliant but disconnected.

The Plugin Bridge

🔐

The Golden Rule

You decide what's connected. ChatGPT cannot reach any system unless you or IT has explicitly authorised that plugin. Your data is only accessible to tools you deliberately unlock.

Three Types of Plugin — Finance Examples

TYPE 1 Data Retrieval ✓ Read-only · Safe

ChatGPT fetches information — it never changes anything.

📊 Pull GL balances📄 Fetch invoice📋 Get budget actuals📅 Read calendar
TYPE 2 Action Plugins ⚠ Human approval gate

ChatGPT initiates an action — you confirm before it executes.

📧 Send email📝 Create event🔔 Trigger approval
TYPE 3 Custom GPTs / GPT Store

Pre-built specialist AI assistants — like downloading a Finance app.

📑 Finance Analyst GPT📊 Excel Formula GPT⚖️ Compliance GPT
🧩

With a Plugin Active

ChatGPT + live connection

Now ChatGPT can ask your ERP for today's AR balance, retrieve a specific invoice, check a customer's payment history, and return a real, current answer — not a guess from training data.
